About us

Welcome to Samatha Apartment & Hotel

Nestled in the heart of this coastal paradise, our hotel and apartment offer a perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and serenity. Immerse yourself in the rich culture and natural wonders of Da Nang. Whether you're strolling along the shores of My Khe Beach, marveling at the intricate architecture of Thien An Pagoda, or hiking the trails of Ngu Hanh Son Mountains, every moment promises to be unforgettable.

Discover the beauty of Da Nang with our hotel strategically located just a short 5-minute walk from the renowned My Khe Beach. Feel the soft sand beneath your feet and indulge in the breathtaking views of the azure sea. Our proximity to stunning landmarks, including Thien An Pagoda and the majestic Ngu Hanh Son Mountains, ensures that your stay is filled with both relaxation and exploration.
